Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man Of India

Here's time line of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

  • 1931:  Born in Rameshwaram, TamilNadu
  • 1954:  Graduated in Physics from University of Madras
  • 1955:  Moved to Madras to study Aerospace Engineering in Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1960:  Graduated from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1965:  Started work on an expandable rocket project independently at DRDO
  • 1969:  Transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • 1980  Successfully deployed the Rohini satellite in near-earth orbit
  • 2002  Won presidential election
  • 2012  launched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement, with a central theme of defeating corruption.
  • 2015  Died

“He is a real source of inspiration for people to achieve big in spite of having born in backward areas with humble beginnings.”
